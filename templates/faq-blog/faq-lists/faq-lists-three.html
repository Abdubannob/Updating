Why did my P score drop?

P score drops by the following measures:

 

Every time you receive a 2 star rating, you lose 15 points

Every time you receive a 1 star rating, you lose 25 points

Every time you cancel a service in progress, you lose 100 points

Every time you cancel a custom order in progress, you lose 100 points

 

You can track your Performance score from the 'P score portal'

How can I increase my P score?

It's highly recommended that you visit 'P score portal' to learn more on how to increase your P score. A quick answer is the following:

 

  • For every quiz assessment you do, you receive 35 points 
  • For every service you create, you get 25 points 
  • Every time you sell a service, you receive 50 points 
  • Every time you win a custom order, you receive 100 points 
  • When you invite another influencer on techfluencer, you receive 200 points
  • Every time you receive a 5 star rating, you receive 25 points
  • Every time you receive a 4 star rating, you receive 15 points
  • Every time you receive a 3 star rating, you receive 5 points

How can brands know if an influencer is a good fit for their campaign?

They can view the influencer's profile, including their P score, niche, audience demographics, and past campaigns, to determine if they are a good fit for their campaign. They can also communicate directly with the influencer to discuss their campaign goals and ensure that they are a good fit.

What is the P score and why is it important for influencers on techfluencer's platform?

P score is a measurement tool used to evaluate the performance of influencers on techfluencer's platform. It is calculated using a variety of metrics, including quiz assessments, service creation, orders delivered, custom order wins, and ratings. A high P score is important for influencers to show how reliable and effecient they are, the higher the better.

How do I earn points towards my P score?

Points can be earned through quiz assessments (35 points), creating services (25 points), selling services (50 points), winning custom orders (100 points), inviting other influencers to the platform (200 points), and receiving high ratings (25 points for 5 star, 15 points for 4 star, 5 points for 3 star).

 

Please visit P score in your dashboard to get a precise report of your performance and possible ways to enhance it.
