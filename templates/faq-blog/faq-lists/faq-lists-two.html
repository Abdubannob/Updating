What is a private delivery?

A private delivery is when an influencer shares their work with a brand before publishing it on social media, to receive approval from the brand. This can include a video for an Instagram story, a post for Facebook, or any other format as specified by the brand's request.

What is a public delivery?

A public delivery is the final submission of an influencer's work, which is shared on social media. This can include a video for an Instagram story, a post for Facebook, or any other format as specified by the brand's request.

What is the process for executing a campaign on techfluencer's platform?

The execution of a campaign involves a series of steps, starting with the brand purchasing a service. The influencer then submits a private delivery for the brand's approval. After the brand approves the private delivery, the influencer submits the social links for the public delivery.

How does the brand approve or reject a private delivery?

The brand has the option to approve or reject a private delivery through the techfluencer platform. Once the brand has reviewed the private delivery, they will be able to provide feedback and make revisions as needed.

Can brands reject a private delivery?

Yes, brands have the option to reject a private delivery from an influencer if it does not meet their needs or expectations. If a private delivery is rejected, the influencer will be notified and given the opportunity to submit a new one. Brands are also expected to provide feedback and explain the reason for the rejection to the influencer.

Why some custom orders receive few applicants sometimes?

If a brand is on the free plan, they will be limited in the number of applicants they can receive per custom order. However, if they are on the premium plan, their custom order will have increased visibility on the influencer marketplace.

What happens if brand rejects a private delivery for 3 times?

If a brand rejects a private delivery 3 times, the influencer will lose points, lose the project, and the brand will get a refund with a 5% charge.

What happens if a brand rejects a private delivery?

If a brand rejects a private delivery, the influencer will have the opportunity to make revisions and resubmit it for approval. If a private delivery is rejected 3 times, the influencer will lose points, lose the project, and the brand will receive a refund with a 5% charge.
