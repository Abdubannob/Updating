What are the differences between the free, Starter, and Premium plans for influencers?

The free plan allows users to connect 3 social media accounts, create up to 8 services, and apply to 10 custom orders per month. The Starter plan allows users to connect unlimited social media accounts, create up to 15 services, apply to 25 custom orders per month, and costs $29/month. The Premium plan allows users to connect unlimited social media accounts, create up to 25 services, apply to 40 custom orders per month, choose 1 service to appear on brands’ top picks collection, and costs $45/month.
