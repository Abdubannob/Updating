How can I list my services on the marketplace?

To list your services on the marketplace, you will need to first connect a social media account, then create a service listing on your influencer profile. The listing should include information about the services you offer, the cost, and any other relevant details.

How to earn money with techfluencer?

There are two ways to earn money through techfluencer:

- Post a service on the marketplace, brands will purchase it

- Apply to custom orders posted by brands on the Custom Order Page, brands will either approve you for the project or select a better match

Can I as an influencer cancel a project in progress?

Yes, but you'll have 50 points deducted from your P score, which results in decreasing the possibility of receiving service purchases

How often can I apply as an influencer for custom orders on techfluencer's platform?

The number of custom orders you can apply for depends on your chosen pricing plan. With the free plan, you can apply to 10 custom orders per month. With the Starter plan, you can apply to 25 custom orders per month. With the Premium plan, you can apply to 40 custom orders per month.

I promote my services on techfluencer?

You can promote your services on techfluencer by regularly updating your profile, listing your services on the marketplace, and applying for custom requests. Additionally, you can promote your techfluencer profile on your own social media channels to attract potential clients.
