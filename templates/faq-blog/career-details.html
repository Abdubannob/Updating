Brands Account Manager

As a Brands Account Manager, you will be responsible for managing and maintaining relationships with a group of brands, often for the duration of their relationship with the company. Your duties will include setting up and onboarding new brands, serving as the primary point of contact for assigned accounts, developing long-term relationships with clients, communicating and interpreting the needs and demands of brands to influencers, forecasting and tracking account metrics, managing projects within brand relationships, identifying opportunities to grow business with existing brands, coordinating with team members working on the same account, collaborating with the sales team to reach prospective clients, and servicing multiple clients concurrently while meeting deadlines.

 

To be successful in this role, you should have experience in account management and client-focused solutions, strong project management skills, excellent negotiation and communication skills, the ability to present effectively, strong time management skills, the ability to communicate client needs with staff, the ability to influence client management, and the ability to manage multiple projects and relationships simultaneously. A bachelor's degree is also required for this position.
